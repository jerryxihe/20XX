Game description:
In the year 20XX, aliens have invaded earth and the human race has nearly been wiped from warfare. You are one of the only fighter pilots still
in service. Your commander Captain Quack has assigned you a difficult mission to destroy the alien forces. You must fight your way through
waves of aliens and defeat their leader, Xerath. Use the left and right arrow keys to control your ship, and press the spacebar to fire your
laser.
